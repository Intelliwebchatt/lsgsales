Who It Fits

Good reps know fast whether a role is real or fluff

Here is the plain-English version. This is a strong fit for people who already know how to present, handle pushback, manage their own energy, and work under a commission structure without falling apart.

Strong fit

  • Big-ticket or commission-only background
  • Comfortable asking for the sale
  • Can run 2 to 3 quality presentations per day
  • Reliable transportation for in-home appointments

Poor fit

  • Needs hourly certainty to function

Common Questions

What candidates usually ask

Do I have to generate my own leads?

No. Appointments are pre-set and credit pre-approved. No cold calling, telemarketing, or door-to-door prospecting is required.

Is this hourly or salary-based?

No. This is a 100% commission-only opportunity. It fits best for people already comfortable with performance-based compensation.

Is there any guaranteed pay?

Yes. There is a guaranteed $25 pitch commission for each completed qualified presentation, whether or not a sale is made.

What backgrounds are strong fits?

Home improvement, automotive, financial services, high-ticket retail, and commission-only contract sales are all strong indicators of fit.

What is the typical commission per deal?

Average commission per closed sale runs about $600 to $700, with deal sizes ranging from $299 to $10,000+ depending on product and conversation.

How quickly will I hear back?

Qualified candidates typically hear back within one business day after review and scheduling.
