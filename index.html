Common Questions

What candidates usually ask.

Do I have to generate my own leads?

No. Appointments are pre-set and credit pre-approved. No cold calling, telemarketing, or door-to-door required.

Is this hourly or salary?

No. This is 100% commission-only. It fits best for people already comfortable with performance-based pay.

Is there any guaranteed pay?

Yes. $25 guaranteed for each completed qualified 90-minute presentation, whether or not a sale is made.

What backgrounds are strong fits?

Home improvement, automotive, financial services, high-ticket retail, and commission-only contract sales.

What is the typical commission per deal?

About $600 to $700 per closed sale, with deal sizes from $299 to $10,000+ depending on product and conversation.

How quickly will I hear back?

Qualified candidates typically hear back within one business day after review and scheduling.
